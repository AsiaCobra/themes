Company Profile

Speed & Ease Logistics Services, Myanmar Customs authorized Agency (licensed no 5204), since 2002. S & E provided customs clearance only in that time. In 2004, S&E has expended itself to provide services in supply chain specialized in customs clearance and transit trade related to logistics services and freight forwarding with the quality services for all customers. In 2013, S & E Logistics is set up as a limited company to provide services widely in the region. S & E is a member of Myanmar Customs Brokers Association and Myanmar International Freight Forwarders Association. It has a good reputation in Myanmar Customs Department and business environment.
